The Best Movies According to Roberto

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Vertigo

A former San Francisco police detective juggles wrestling with his personal demonsand becoming

obsessed with the hauntingly beautiful woman he has been hired to trail, who may be deeply disturbed.

La Dolce Vita

A series of stories following a week in the life of a philandering tabloid journalist living in Rome.

The Matrix (franchise)

When a beautiful stranger leads computer hacker Neo to a forbidding underworld, he discovers the shocking truth.

The life he knows is the elaborate deception of an evil cyber-intelligence.
